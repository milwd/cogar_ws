Actuation & Control Components

encoder_arm.py

ROS node that simulates the arm encoder by publishing random encoder readings. Publishes Int32 messages on ‘/encoder_arm’ at 10 Hz for testing downstream controllers.

encoder_arm.arm_encoder()[source]

Initialize the ROS node and publish simulated arm encoder values.

Process

  1. Initialize ROS node ‘encoder_arm’.

  2. Create a Publisher on ‘/encoder_arm’ for Int32 messages.

  3. Loop at 10 Hz until shutdown: a. Generate a random float between 0 and 5. b. Wrap in Int32 and publish. c. Sleep to maintain loop rate.
